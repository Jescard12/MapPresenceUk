Surgical Specialties

Dr. Awad offers a comprehensive range of surgical services, bringing years of expertise and advanced techniques to every procedure.

🏥

General Surgery

Comprehensive general surgical care covering a broad spectrum of conditions, from routine procedures to complex interventions requiring expert surgical judgment.

🫃

Abdominal Surgery

Specialized surgical treatment of abdominal organs including the stomach, intestines, liver, gallbladder, and other digestive system structures.

🎗️

Mammary Surgery

Expert breast surgery services including diagnostic and therapeutic procedures, performed with sensitivity and precision to achieve the best possible outcomes.

🔬

Oncosurgery

Surgical oncology focused on the removal of cancerous tumors and affected tissues, coordinated with comprehensive cancer treatment plans.

🦋

Thyroid Surgery

Precise thyroid and parathyroid surgical procedures including thyroidectomy, treating nodules, hyperthyroidism, cancer, and other thyroid-related conditions.

🔭

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive laparoscopic techniques that reduce recovery time, minimize scarring, and lower complication rates compared to open surgery approaches.
